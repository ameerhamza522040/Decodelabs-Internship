Who We Are

A small team, obsessed with craft.

Founded in 2019, Nexora Digital is a boutique studio of designers and engineers who believe great software is built at the intersection of clarity, performance, and empathy for the end user. We don't do templates — every layout is engineered for the content it carries.

  • Semantic, accessible HTML5 by default
  • Mobile-first, fluid responsive systems
  • Performance budgets on every build
  • WCAG 2.1 AA compliance as a baseline
